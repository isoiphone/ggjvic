WHAT WILL BE YOUR FATE!?!


CONTROLS
========
Launch game with a 2-axis joystick connected.

First Axis: move
Second Axis: aim
Button: SPEAR!

Alternately, launch game with no joystick.

WASD or ARROWS: move
mouse: aim (relative to centre of screen)
space OR click: fire
